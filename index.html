About our company

Lol art company was founded in 2019. The main direction of our activity is organizing international supplies of a wide range of goods at the request of our clients. Over the years, we have accumulated extensive experience in searching for suppliers, conducting legal and financial audits of potential transactions, and directly organizing the supplies themselves.

If you have a need to purchase technological equipment, textiles, spare parts for equipment anywhere in the world, but do not have experience or confidence in choosing the right supplier, contact us. Our team is ready to solve the most complex problems, while remaining socially responsible and highly valuing environmental standards.

Our main services

Searching for a supplier according to your request, conducting negotiations, monitoring the compliance of the transaction parameters with your request.

Conducting a legal assessment of the transaction. We will offer optimal financial and logistics tools for organizing delivery.

If you already know what and where you want to buy, we will be happy to organize a full cycle of delivery of any types of goods to the place you need. We will monitor compliance with all legal requirements along the entire cargo route.
